Instructions

  1. Left-click anywhere to create a new particle
  2. Left-click and drag to give that particle a velocity vector
  3. Right-click and drag to move the camera or use the arrow keys
  4. Middle-click any particle to center the camera on it
  5. Press C to center on the most massive particle, or to disable centering
  6. Use the mouse wheel to zoom in out or use Z and X
  7. Modify the sliders in the sidebar
  8. Have fun
